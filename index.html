“Born to BEE Wild”

"Barry B. Benson, a bee just graduated from college, is disillusioned at his lone career choice: making honey. On a special trip outside the hive, Barry's life is saved by Vanessa, a florist in New York City. As their relationship blossoms, he discovers humans actually eat honey, and subsequently decides to sue them." -IMDB

To this day I remember riding my bike to the movie theater in 2007 just to see this movie. Since then, I have seen it at least once a week for the last 13 years and there’s always something new to see or a new joke to appreciate. You have to think bee-sh in order to fully get this completely underrated movie. What starts out as a fun movie regarding a bee’s life about him finally getting to select his forever job, leads to the huge discovery that humans have been stealing honey and what’s the best way to handle that?? By suing the human race! There’s romance, action, comedy, and an underlying plot that our world couldn’t survive without bees!!
